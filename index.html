ABOUT ME

Hello there!

I'm Dave, an outdoor and fashion photographer based in Keffi, Nasarawa state.
"Photography is my muse, my true profession, and the thing that will undoubtedly drive me insane someday"
I don't just photograph people. I capture the way they make me feel. The emotions... I'll admit its a bit of a strange concept as I wrestle with every image I shoot. That's a great feeling to be honest... Just as our cultures are different, I love to capture them differently too. From the elegant Yoruba style to the classy Hausa tribe and the fabulous Igbo wedding. All of it is what I simply want to capture!
